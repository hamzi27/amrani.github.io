I am looking for a career where I can work to help people, contribute to enhancing modern technologies in Artificial Intelligence, and strive for high-quality projects while improving my knowledge and my skills.

Currently, I am working as a Data Scientist at ContinuumLab AI, a Venture Studio in San Francisco that builds AI start-ups and AI applications for business. I am helping develop the Luminous Startup for Sustainability Innovation by analysing and applying AI and NLP approaches to Environment Social and Governance data.

I did my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at the University of Milan-Bicocca working on Recognition and Retrieval of logos from digital images with Prof. Raimondo Schettini and Dr. Marco Buzzelli. Then, I pursued MSc in Computer Science, with a focus on Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Signal Processing. My master's thesis (view here) was about the Personalization of Models for Human Activity Recognition with Deep Learning approaches under the supervision of Prof. Paolo Napoletano and Prof. Daniela Micucci.
My research interests include Machine Learning and Deep Learning, Data Science, Signal Processing, Computer Vision and Unsupervised Learning.
